1) peptide sequences of two species in fasta format
For example (downloaded from www.phytozome.net):
Osativa_204_peptide.fa
Zmays_181_peptide.fa

2) GFF3 files of two species
For example (downloaded from www.phytozome.net):
Osativa_204_gene.gff3
Zmays_181_gene.gff3

3) output file from BBH-LS pipleine
For example (in test directory):
ZM-OS_BBH-LS.spairs

4) sample test dataset from Zea mays
maize-test_1.fq
maize-test_2.fq

bowtie
CLUSTALW
